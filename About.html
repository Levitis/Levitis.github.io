About Me

Welcome to my blog! I'm Levitis Lorenzo Burke-Astorino, and I'm excited to share my journey with you—a journey shaped by my unique upbringing, diverse experiences, and my passion for technology, finance, and sports.

Born as an only child to two fully deaf parents—my father, an Italian immigrant, and my mother, a second-generation Jamaican—I grew up in the vibrant communities of South London, moving from Brixton to Croydon. My early life was a blend of rich cultural influences and challenges that instilled in me a strong sense of determination and resilience.

My passion for technology began in childhood, with a dream of becoming a game developer. Though I initially pursued a career in finance, gaining significant experience such as managing a £600 million fund during my internship at Octopus Energy, my love for tech remained strong. This passion has led me to the exciting field of FinTech, where I aim to blend my financial expertise with innovative technology.

Sports have also played a pivotal role in my life. I played rugby for Harlequins until the age of 16 before switching to American football, where I was selected for the Team GB under-19 squad and won two national championships while captaining the London Blitz. Today, I continue to pursue my love for the sport as a Safety for the University of Nottingham's first team.

Through this blog, I share insights from my journey across finance, technology, and sports. Join me as I explore the intersection of these fields and how they shape the world we live in.
